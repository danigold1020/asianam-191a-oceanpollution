Welcome to our mapplication!


Hello! We are a group of three UCLA students enrolled in Asian American Studies 191A, a Web Development and GIS for Social Change class. We created this survey for the Santa Barbara community in California to learn about local residents' experiences and civic engagement (public hearings, town hall meetings, etc.) regarding their close proximity to oil spills and the barriers and challenges they have faced in trying to participate in civic engagement. If you are apart of the Santa Barbara community feel free to take our survey to contribute! Or navigate our map to see fellow community members' experiences and/or barriers to civic engagement regarding oil spills.

What has it been like to participate in civic engagement regarding oil spills in the Santa Barbara community?

Explore the filters on the map to read the positive, neutral, and negative experiences as well as the struggles of those who are unable to participate due to challenges with accessibility.
