Welcome to our mapplication! The Oilies

Hello! We are a group of three UCLA students enrolled in Asian American Studies 191A, a Web Development and GIS for Social Change class. We created this survey for the Santa Barbara community in California to provide a platform for local residents' to share and hear about experiences in civic engagement involving oil spills or the barriers and challenges they have faced in trying to participate in civic engagement.
