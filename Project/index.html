Welcome to our mapplication!

Hello! We are a group of three UCLA students enrolled in Asian American Studies 191A, a Web Development and GIS for Social Change class. We created this survey for the Santa Barbara community in California to learn about local residents' experiences in civic engagement involving oil spills or the barriers and challenges they have faced in trying to participate in civic engagement.
