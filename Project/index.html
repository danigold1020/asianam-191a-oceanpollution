Welcome to our mapplication!

Hello! We are a group of three UCLA students enrolled in Asian American Studies 191A, a Web Development and GIS for Social Change class. We created this survey for the Santa Barbara community in California to provide a platform for local residents' to share and hear about experiences in civic engagement regarding oil spills or the barriers and challenges they have faced in trying to participate in civic engagement.

How to navigate our mapplication:
-> Explore our map by clicking on the legend in the top right corner to filter survey responses based on positive, negative, and neutral experiences
-> Examine the polygons on the map to see how respondents feel in each zip code region of Santa Barbara County
-> Scroll through the responses based on experience type to learn more about the thoughts and experiences of Santa Barbara residents regarding civic engagement and oil spills

Click on a region to read different stories!
