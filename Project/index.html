Welcome to our mapplication!

Hello! We are a group of three UCLA students enrolled in Asian American Studies 191A, a Web Development and GIS for Social Change class. Our project aims to uplift residents of Santa Barbara, California by providing a platform for local residents to share stories of their experiences in civic engagement regarding oil spills and the barriers they have faced in trying to do so. We invite you to navigate through our map to read stories of Santa Barbara community members or to share your own story.

How to navigate our mapplication:
→ Click on the legend in the top right corner to filter survey responses based on positive, negative, and neutral experiences, as well as those with no experience.
→ Examine the polygons on the map to see how respondents feel in each zip code region of Santa Barbara County
→ Scroll through the responses based on experience type to learn more about the thoughts and experiences of Santa Barbara residents

Click on a region to read different stories!
