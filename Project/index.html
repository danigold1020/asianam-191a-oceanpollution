Welcome to our mapplication!


Hello! We are a group of three UCLA students enrolled in Asian American Studies 191A, a Web Development and GIS for Social Change class. We created this survey for the Santa Barbara community in California to learn about local residents' experiences and civic engagement involving oil spills and the barriers and challenges they have faced in trying to participate in civic engagement. We created our project for the residents of the Santa Barbara community that live in close proximity to oil wells and have experienced the destruction of oil spills. If you are apart of the Santa Barbara community feel free to take our survey to contribute! Or navigate our map to see fellow community members' experiences and barriers to civic engagement regarding oil spills.
